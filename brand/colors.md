## üí° Usage Guidelines

### Do's ‚úÖ
- Use Brand Blue for primary actions and links
- Use semantic colors for their intended purpose
- Maintain sufficient contrast for accessibility
- Use neutral colors for text and backgrounds

### Don'ts ‚ùå
- Don't use multiple primary colors in the same context
- Don't use low-contrast color combinations
- Don't modify brand colors without approval
- Don't use semantic colors decoratively
